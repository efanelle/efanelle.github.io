Booze and Books Creators

Booze and Books was created by 3 sisters with a love of reading and a fine cocktail. They worked together to create an environment that is conducive to many lifestyles. They always loved hanging out and having cocktails together, and they wanted to tie that together with their love of reading. After lot of discussion, Booze and Books was born.
