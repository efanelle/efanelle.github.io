Booze and Books is a group dedicated to providing people with good books and great cocktails. Going to a "Book Club" is a great idea (in theory). Unfortunately, not everyone has the luxury of being able to leave their house once or twice a month.

THAT'S WHERE WE COME IN!

Our main goal is to to provide good book suggestions and great cocktails to go with them. Books can take us to many different places, and what better way to enjoy that trip than with the appropriate beverage to accompany you. Reading a heavy, emotionally-filled book like Moby Dick could require a cocktail like a Dark and Stormy, while a fun read such as Mindy Kaling's Is Everyone Hanging out without Me? would pair great with a crisp glass of Chardonnay.

Join us as we launch our online book club communuity. We will provide great books and even better cocktails. Please visit our website at www.boozeandbooks.com

We want to keep this a very positive and upbeat environment, so we ask that you join this group only if you are an avid reader (or are looking to become one) and are looking to have a good time. We ask that you do your very best to finish each book before the "meeting" so you are able to participate in the dicussion and review of the book. Our main goal is to learn about new books, great cocktails, and to have fun.
