First Entry: WELCOME!

April 14, 2016

Booze and Books is officially up and running! We would like to personally welcome everyone to our booze-filled book club. On the first of each month we will be posting our new book and drink on the site. Our discussion board will be up as soon as our first book and drink are posted. Look for the announcement on May 1st! We look forward to this new club and can't wait to get started with our reading and drinking!

If you have any suggestions for cocktails or books in the meantime, we would love to hear from you. Feel free to email us with any ideas you might have!
